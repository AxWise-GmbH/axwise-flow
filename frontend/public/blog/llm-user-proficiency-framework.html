Understanding AI Proficiency in the Modern Era

As Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini become increasingly integrated into our daily workflows, understanding different levels of user proficiency has become crucial. Whether you're a complete beginner or an advanced practitioner, knowing where you stand can help guide your learning journey and career development.

This comprehensive framework identifies 13 distinct levels of LLM user proficiency, from "The Uninitiated" who have never directly interacted with AI, to "The Pinnacle User" who architects cutting-edge AI systems. Each level represents a significant step in understanding and capability.
